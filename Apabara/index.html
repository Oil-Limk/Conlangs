Apabara

Apabara aims to be an IAL acting global pidgin language. Apabara strives to be a simple and rich language, guided by (kin) basic principles.

Principles

If you can understand it, it's Apabara

Apabara encompasses any form of communication that is understood, both by its sender and receiver. This means that krokodili1 occurs when this condition is not met. Thus, Apabara incentivizes the use of a widely known vocabulary and in situations where one or more languages (of any procedence) are understood by everyone in said communicational situation, the use of said languages is encouraged. Only in situations where there's no language in common, the use of a more "apabaric" approach is suggested.
